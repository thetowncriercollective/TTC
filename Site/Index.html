About Us

We are a Trademark for audio visual art, working through the collaboration of creatives to optimise production and publication of top quality audio-visual art.

It is our purpose that we push the boundaries of innovation in the live music scene through technical skill and detailed concepts All the while building a community that trusts in our process so much so that our label on any project guarantees them of an exceptional listening and watching experience

For the Creatives

We are a team made by of and for the creatives, we understand what it means to be a modern day artist in the real and digital world.

That being said, it is our primary goal that through the support, collaboration and supervision within our team we enable the growth of all creatives and artists working with us .

We have a harmonious relationship within the team(externally and internally) sharing a common goal and that is the production of high quality art.

We believe strongly that the art should grow the artist and that is our priority, Growth.
